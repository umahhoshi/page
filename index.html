Umah Hoshi 

Umah Hoshi

One of our top picks in Tegalalang.Situated in Tegalalang, 6 km from Tegallalang Rice Terrace, Umah Hoshi features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar. With a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, every room has a patio with pool view. At Umah Hoshi the rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast.

Umah Hoshi offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel.

Ceking Ricefield Terrace is 5 km from Umah Hoshi. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 57 km from the accommodation.
